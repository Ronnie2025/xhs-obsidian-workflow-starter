# Sample Output Structure

Recommended archive structure for one Xiaohongshu post package:

```text
小红书工作流/
  发布包/
    2026-06-16_Obsidian素材变发布包_3x4/
      00-source.md
      01-brief.md
      02-title-candidates.md
      03-body-draft.md
      04-carousel-plan.md
      05-image-prompts.md
      06-final-post.md
      07-publish-order.md
      images/
        01-cover.png
        02-problem.png
        03-method.png
        04-example.png
        05-boundary.png
      manifest.json
```

Why this structure works:

- The source remains visible.
- The title decision can be reviewed later.
- Image prompts stay connected to the final images.
- The final publishing text is separate from intermediate drafts.
- The package can be reused as a future reference.
