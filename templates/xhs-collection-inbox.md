## Sorting Rule

Use three buckets only:

- usable: can become a post or source card within 7 days
- reference: useful idea, but needs more proof or examples
- discard: vague, duplicated, outdated, or not aligned with your account

## Promotion Check

Promote a source card into `templates/xhs-post-brief.md` only when it has:

- A specific reader.
- A real pain point.
- A reusable method or example.
- A title angle.
- A boundary statement.

## Boundary

This template is for organizing notes you are allowed to save and reuse. It is not a crawler, not account operation service, and not a promise of views, followers, sales, or income.
