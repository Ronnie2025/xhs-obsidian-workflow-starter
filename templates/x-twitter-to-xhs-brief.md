## Review Checklist

- [ ] I linked or recorded the original public source.
- [ ] I rewrote the idea in my own words.
- [ ] I added my own context, example, or decision rule.
- [ ] The reader problem is specific.
- [ ] The title does not overpromise.
- [ ] The post does not imply guaranteed followers, traffic, sales, income, or payback.
- [ ] Private account information, cookies, internal files, API keys, and paywalled content are removed.
- [ ] The final package can be found later from the Obsidian archive folder.
